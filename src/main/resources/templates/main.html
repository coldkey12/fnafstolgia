Fredbear's Family Diner

A Fazbear Entertainment Subsidiary

Welcome to Fredbear's Family Diner! As one of the earliest family entertainment centers developed by Fazbear Entertainment, Fredbear's offered a magical experience for children and adults alike. Founded in 1983, our beloved animatronic characters, Fredbear and Spring Bonnie, performed live music and hosted parties every day of the week.

Fredbear's Family Diner was built on the promise of creating a safe, fun, and interactive dining experience. It laid the groundwork for future developments such as Freddy Fazbear's Pizza, which has since expanded across the country under the guidance of Fazbear Entertainment's corporate leadership.

Safety is our top priority. While the location is no longer open to the public, we continue to maintain the facility and invest in ongoing security and preservation as part of our historical division. All operations are in full compliance with Fazbear Entertainment standards and guidelines.
