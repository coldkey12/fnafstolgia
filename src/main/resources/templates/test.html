The Five Nights at Freddy's (FNaF) franchise is a multimedia horror phenomenon created by Scott Cawthon, who released the first game on August 8, 2014. Known for its chilling atmosphere, eerie animatronics, and deeply hidden lore, FNaF quickly became a cultural icon in indie gaming.

The games are set in various locations owned by Fazbear Entertainment, from Fredbear's Family Diner in 1983 to Freddy Fazbear's Mega Pizzaplex in the latest installment. Each title peels back the layers of mystery surrounding missing children, haunted animatronics, and the legacy of the elusive William Afton.

Beyond the games, FNaF has expanded into novels, fan-made games, a movie adaptation, and an expansive online fan community obsessed with theories and timeline connections.
